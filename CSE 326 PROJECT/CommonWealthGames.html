The Modern Commonwealth Game's HISTORY

The common wealth Games is an international multi-sport event involving athelets from common wealth nations. the event was first held in 1930 and has taken place for every four years since then. The Common wealth Games were known as The British Empire Games from 1930 to 1950, whereas it is also the worlds first multi-sport event which iducts equal number of Men & Women medal events and was implemented recently in 2018 Common Wealth Games with such a unique features the World Economic Forum called the event inspiring and significant.
